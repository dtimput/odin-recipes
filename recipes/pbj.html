Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

A childhood classic! Incase you've never made any food in your life on your own, heres an easy to follow recipe so you can make your own!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread, usually white bread
  • Peanut Butter, Chunky or Smooth
  • Jelly, preferably grape

Steps

  1. Using a butter knife, spread peanut butter on one side of a single piece of bread
  2. Clean the knife, and then use it to spread jelly on one side of the other piece of bread
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together, with the Peanut butter and Jelly sides touching, and enjoy!
